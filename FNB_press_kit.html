DIRECTOR'S STATEMENT

This film is an homage to the genre, I am very passionate about Noir. From the visuals and the acting, it is to deliver a stirring film. As a filmmaker, representation is important and I hold diversity to a usual standard. On this and previous projects, I have always employed cast and crew, of diverse ethnicity. I think my films should reflect how diverse America is and everyone should feel represented.“False and Baseless” is a drama with a female-forward narrative. This story is essential for our community to see and talk about.

WRITER'S STATEMENT

My fascination with the crime movie genre is due to the intense feeling I get when I watch crime-based narratives. I wanted to write a screenplay that provides the same feelings. Then I saw a documentary about a serial rapist, and from the documentary, what bothered me the most is that a victim had to deal with harsh questioning from the police. I had that same uncomfortable feeling that I wanted for my film. I researched how often women deal with "secondary victimization" from testing about their rape. Unfortunately, it does happen more than people think. I believe my film presents a female voice that is fighting to be heard and believed. I want to empower women not to remain silent for the convenience of a flawed justice system. Talking about sexual assault is still stigmatized, and I hope this film changes that.

ABOUT THE FILM

Logline

In Austin, Texas in a police interrogation room, a female victim is being interviewed by a detective about the night she was raped. The detective questions the victim’s credibility.

Tagline

She is determined to testify about her sexual assault because she deserves to be believed.

Synopsis

A college student is a victim of rape and during her statement to the police detective, the victim's statement goes from empathy to confrontational.
